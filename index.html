Spinach, Tofu and Sesame Stir-Fry

By: Martha Rose Shulman

Yield: 3 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • ½ pound tofu, cut in small dice
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated or minced fresh ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • 16-ounce bag baby spinach, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Preparation

Step 1

Heat the canola oil over medium-high heat in a large nonstick skillet or wok, and add the tofu. Stir-fry until the tofu is lightly colored (three to five minutes), then add the garlic, ginger, and chili flakes. Cook, stirring, until fragrant (about one minute), and add soy sauce to taste. Add the spinach and stir-fry until the spinach wilts (about one minute). Stir in the sesame seeds, add more soy sauce if desired, and remove from the heat.

Step 2

Using tongs, transfer the spinach and tofu mixture to a serving bowl, leaving the liquid behind in the pan. Drizzle with the sesame oil, add more soy sauce as desired, and serve with rice, other grains, noodles, or even as a pita bread filling.

Tip

Advance preparation: This is a last-minute preparation, but you can have your ingredients prepared well in advance.
